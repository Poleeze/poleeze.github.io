Taboo

The topic of money is something rarely talked about at dinner tables, either at home or at formal events. A few topics are almost a taboo to discuss in public. They are money, religion and politics. These topics seem to be very sensitive topics that can get people flying off the handle pretty quickly. I can understand if some people distance themselves from politics and religion, but amuses me is people trying not to talk about money. What is even bigger of an amusement is that everybody has an opinion. The rich, the poor, the middle class. And they all believe their opinions are valid. Don't get me wrong, there's something you can learn from all three classes of people regarding money, the important thing is to learn the right stuff.
